Installing Template.

  1. After unzip the download pack, you'll found a Template Folder with all the files.
  2. You can view this Template in any browser, you can display or edit the Template without an internet connection.(May not wotrk fonts).
  3. The only section that will not work is the Contact Section which is not dynamic
  4. Now go to your cpanel or open your FTP Client (like Filezilla) and upload the content of the Template on your server's root directory.
  5. Once the files are done uploading go to www.yourdomainname.com/index.html
